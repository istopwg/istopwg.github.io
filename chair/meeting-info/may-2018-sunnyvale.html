I. When/Where

This PWG face-to-face meeting will be held on May 15 - 17, 2018, hosted by the PWG at the Wild Palms Hotel, 910 E Fremont Ave, Sunnyvale, CA 94087 [map]

A light breakfast and lunch will be served each day.

All sessions will be held in the "Cobalt Room" conference room. Attendees should enter via the Main Lobby for directions to the room.


II. Registration/RSVP

There is no registration fee for this meeting.

Please respond to the PWG May 2018 Face-to-Face Meeting Survey by April 15, 2018 whether you are planning to attend the meeting in person, by phone, or not at all. This will help us plan for services and make any necessary reservations.
